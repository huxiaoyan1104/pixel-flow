# Responsible Use

Pixel Flow is an image workflow tool. It does not grant copyright permissions or override website terms. You are responsible for confirming whether you may access, download, organize, export, and reuse the relevant images.

## Boundaries

- Do not bypass website access restrictions.
- Do not download or reuse images you are not authorized to process.
- Do not treat technical inferences as legal, ownership, or authenticity conclusions.
- Before sharing export files, review source URLs, tags, account information, and project context.

## Team Guidance

If your team uses Pixel Flow for client work, ecommerce, AI datasets, or public content, define who checks authorization, who exports records, and who approves final publishing.
