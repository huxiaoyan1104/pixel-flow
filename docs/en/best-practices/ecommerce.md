# Ecommerce Best Practices

Ecommerce operators can use Pixel Flow to inspect product page images, thumbnails, responsive sources, and download records.

## Recommended Flow

1. Open a product detail page.
2. Scan page images.
3. Filter by dimensions, format, and source to find main images, detail images, and thumbnails.
4. Save candidate assets and apply product or campaign tags.
5. Review image details to confirm dimensions and available metadata.
6. Export records for operations, design, or merchandising review.

## Signals to Watch

- Whether main image dimensions are consistent.
- Whether detail images have multiple versions.
- Whether thumbnails and large images share the same source.
- Whether download history can be traced to the page.

## Important Note

Competitor images usually cannot be reused directly. Pixel Flow helps review and organize; it does not replace permission checks.
