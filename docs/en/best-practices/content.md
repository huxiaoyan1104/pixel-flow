# Content Team Best Practices

Content teams can use Pixel Flow to manage article image candidates, campaign assets, social visual references, and download records.

## Recommended Flow

1. Open a source, brand, or asset page.
2. Scan the current page images.
3. Filter out images that are too small or have unclear sources.
4. Save candidate images and tag them by column, campaign, or publish date.
5. Export image records for editorial or design review.

## Tagging Ideas

- `article-candidate`
- `campaign`
- `needs-license-check`
- `approved-source`

## Before Publishing

Confirm image permission, attribution requirements, portrait rights, and brand restrictions. Export records can support internal review, but they are not license files.
